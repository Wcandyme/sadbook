How to Join the Sadbook Webcomic Webring

Criteria to join

  • You must be an indie webcomic or comic strip published online.
  • NSFW content should not be the focal point of the webcomic/comic strip.
  • No hate speech or bigotry.
  • Having ads is ok, being nothing but ads is not ok.
It is impossible to enumerate everything that is not allowed. For this reason, we (the webmasters) reserve the right to remove a site if we feel it is outside of our own personal bounds.
